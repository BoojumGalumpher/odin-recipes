Condensed Jubjub

The meal of choice of the daring Jabberwock slayer and the enterprising Snark hunter

Best kept in ivory jars, or in mahogany kegs.

Ingredients

  • Tape
  • Sawdust
  • Glue
  • Locusts
  • Jubjub

Steps

Note: The Jubjub's symmetrical shape must be preserved.

  1. Boil Jubjub
  2. Salt Jubjub in glue
  3. Condense with locusts and tape
