Fruit Fractal

A fractal fruit and vegetable salad, for those with fractal stomachs

The ideal meal for the gargantuan appetite. Best served fresh and under an open sky. Best assimilated if consumed meditatively.

Ingredients

  • Mangos
  • Kiwis
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumbers
  • Concord grapes
  • Bananas
  • Pineapples
  • Raspberries
  • Blackberries
  • Tangerines
  • Guavas
  • Avocados

Steps

Note: Wash thoroughly with water and apple vinegar. Peel if needed/desired. The radius of the circle is six meters (r = 6m). The angle between the circle and the ground is either 0 degrees or 90 degrees. The latter is more challenging, but more rewarding. Move clockwise. Begin at twelve o'clock.

  1. Fill the first hour with tomatoes
  2. Fill the second hour with raspberries
  3. Fill the third hour with tangerines
  4. Fill the fourth hour with mangos
  5. Fill the fifth hour with bananas
  6. Fill the sixth hour with pineapples
  7. Fill the seventh hour with guavas
  8. Fill the eighth hour with cucumbers
  9. Fill the ninth hour with avocados
  10. Fill the tenth hour with kiwis
  11. Fill the eleventh hour with grapes
  12. Fill the twelfth hour with blackberries
  13. Move back 6 meters
  14. Start over from step 1
